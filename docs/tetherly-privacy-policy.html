Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Neha Pathmanaban ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in the Tetherly app ("the App"). Tetherly is a multi-tenant wedding companion app that couples use to share logistics, photos, and messages with their guests.

Information We Collect

From guests using a wedding's app

  • Your name (used to log in and identify you within the app)
  • Contact information (phone number and email address, if you choose to provide them)
  • Dietary restrictions and food preferences
  • Travel information (hotel, check-in/out dates, arrival time, flight number)
  • Push notification device tokens (to send you wedding updates)
  • Song requests
  • Message reactions and replies
  • Packing list status
  • Photos you choose to upload
  • Questions you ask the in-app AI assistant, and the responses returned to you (stored so you can revisit them in the History view; you can delete individual entries or clear all of them at any time from within the assistant)

From couples requesting a Tetherly app

If you submit the in-app request form to set up a new wedding on Tetherly, we collect:

  • Couple name(s)
  • Wedding date (or date range, for multi-day celebrations)
  • Your email address
  • City or venue (optional)
  • Free-form notes you choose to include (optional)

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Personalize your experience in the App
  • Help coordinate wedding logistics (meals, travel, music)
  • Send push notifications with wedding updates and reminders
  • Share memories among wedding guests
  • Generate AI assistant responses to your questions about the wedding, drawing on the wedding's published details (schedule, dress codes, packing list, destination guide) plus general knowledge
  • Allow the couple hosting your wedding to review what guests are asking, so they can update the app's information accordingly
  • Respond to couples who request a new Tetherly app for their wedding (via email)

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose. We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Service Providers

We use a small number of third-party services to operate Tetherly. They process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

  • Supabase — database, file storage (for photos), and serverless functions. All data is stored on encrypted servers.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (used to notify us when a couple submits the Tetherly request form, and, once a verified sender domain is in place, to send the couple a confirmation of their submission). Also used to forward AI-response reports submitted via the in-app "Report" button to the couple hosting the wedding.
  • Anthropic — AI processing for the in-app assistant. When you ask a question, the question, the wedding's published details (schedule, dress codes, packing list, destination guide), and a small profile tag (your guest name and role, e.g. "wedding party") are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. Anthropic does not train its models on data submitted via the API and retains submissions only briefly for abuse monitoring. See Anthropic's privacy policy for details.
  • Apple Push Notification service — delivery of push notifications to iOS devices.

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

Data Retention

Guest data is retained for the duration of each wedding's event period and a reasonable window afterward, then deleted. Wedding-request submissions are retained until we have followed up and either set up the requested app or closed out the request. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

Children's Privacy

Tetherly is intended for use by adult wedding guests and couples and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date above.
