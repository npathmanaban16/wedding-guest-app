Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Neha Pathmanaban ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in the Neha & Naveen Wedding App ("the App").

Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Dietary restrictions and food preferences
  • Travel information (hotel and flight details)
  • Song requests
  • Packing list status
  • Photos you choose to upload

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Personalise your experience in the App
  • Help coordinate wedding logistics (meals, travel, music)
  • Share memories among wedding guests

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database service. Supabase stores data on encrypted servers. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

Data Retention

Your data will be retained for the duration of the wedding period and deleted thereafter. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by wedding guests and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
