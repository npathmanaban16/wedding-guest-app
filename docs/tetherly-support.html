Tetherly Support

A wedding companion app for couples and their guests.

About the app

Tetherly brings everything your wedding guests need into one elegant app — the schedule, travel guide, photos, packing list, messages, and music requests — all accessible with a private invite code from the couple hosting the wedding.

Common questions

I can't find my invite code

Invite codes are issued by the couple hosting the wedding. Please reach out to them directly — they'll be able to share it with you.

The app says it can't find my name on the guest list

Double-check the spelling of the name you entered. Names are matched against the list the couple provided, so if the couple knows you as "Liz Smith" but you typed "Elizabeth Smith", it won't match. Contact the couple if you're still stuck — they can update the list.

I'd like to try Tetherly before signing up for my wedding

On the invite screen, tap "Just curious? Try the demo →" below the main form. You'll be signed into a sample wedding where you can explore all the tabs without committing to anything.

I'm a couple — how do I set up a wedding on Tetherly?

On the invite screen, tap "Getting married? Set up your wedding →". Fill out the short form with your names, date, and email, and we'll get back to you within a few days to configure your wedding.

How do I sign out?

Open the Details tab (the last tab in the bottom bar) and scroll to the bottom. You'll find a Sign out link there. On the preview/demo account, you can also tap the "Preview mode · Tap to exit →" banner at the top of any screen.
